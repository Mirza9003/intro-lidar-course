# Introduction to Basic Concepts of LiDAR

**Author:** Mirza Md Tasnim Mukarram  
**Affiliation:** University of Iowa  
**License:** CC BY 4.0  

This open course introduces the physical principles, sensor systems, data products, and environmental applications of LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging). The material integrates conceptual explanations, figures, interactive quizzes, and applied examples relevant to geospatial science, hydrology, and environmental monitoring.

By completing this course, learners will be able to:

- Explain the operating principles of active remote sensing systems.
- Quantify LiDAR ranging using time-of-flight equations.
- Distinguish airborne, terrestrial, and bathymetric LiDAR platforms.
- Interpret point cloud attributes and derived products.
- Evaluate LiDAR suitability for hydrologic and environmental applications.
